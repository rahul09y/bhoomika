I’m Sorry, Bhoomika

From my heart, just for you...

I whispered "sorry" to the wind,
Hoping somehow it finds you.
Not just for a word I didn’t say,
But for the love I failed to show you too.

Regret lingers in every silent night,
And echoes loud in dreams I see,
All I want is one more chance,
To love you right, endlessly.
